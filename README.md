# # Sociology Archive  
Curated by **Dr. Fariborz Aref**  

---

### Overview  
**Sociology Archive** is a living repository of research, teaching, and analytical materials across the full spectrum of sociology.  
It integrates **quantitative modeling**, **theoretical insight**, and **public discourse**, documenting how societies evolve through inequality, development, and transformation.

This archive serves as both a **record and resource** — connecting data, theory, and lived experience.  
It reflects over two decades of academic and field-based work in **inequality research, social structure, community development, and transnational sociology.**

---

### Core Areas
| Domain | Focus |
|---------|-------|
| **Social Inequality** | Cross-national measures of income, health, and labor inequality |
| **Community Development** | Policy design, sustainability, and local empowerment |
| **Quantitative Sociology** | Statistical modeling (GEE, SEM, LMM) and data reproducibility |
| **Sociological Theory** | Classical and modern thought on structure, agency, and modernity |
| **Social Justice & Policy** | Institutional accountability and human rights frameworks |
| **Pedagogy & Curriculum** | Teaching resources for research methods and applied sociology |
